PROBE_10PX_TTF Bold Version 1.000

Description: PROBE_10PX Bold is a free-to-use non-commercial 10:11 bitmap-based font created by Andrew Sigurow (http://www.facebook.com/andrewsigurow) and released under the SIL Open Font License (OFL) (http://scripts.sil.org/OFL).

Features:
- OpenType TrueType;
- base glyphs are 10:11;
- no subscript/superscript;
- no strikeout;

Panose:
- Latin Decorative;
- derivative;
- medium weight;
- monospaced;
- low contrast;
- normal sans serif;
- standard solid fill;
- no lining;
- standard topology;
- extended characters range;

Supported codepages:
- 1252, Latin-1;
- 1250, Latin-2 (Eastern Europe);
- 1251, Cyrillic;
- 1253, Greek;
- 1254, Turkish;
- 1257, Windows Baltic;
- Mac Roman;

Usage:
- use at 10pt (or multiple of 10);
- anti-alias off;
